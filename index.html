Top 5 Tech Podcast Episodes

Perhaps a slight bias towards Software Engineering Daily!

  • Facebook Developers with Nick Schrock | The early years at Facebook. Monolithic release to production every Tuesday, co-ordinated by IRC!
  • PlayStation Engineering with Tony Godar | Great to hear the history this ecosystem, more-so having spent significant time as an end-user
  • RocksDB with Dhruba Borthakur and Igor Canadi | I enjoyed the wider discussion on Storage Engines and the comparison between BTree and LSM Trees
  • Monica Beckwith on Java Garbage Collection | Great overview of GC
  • Distributed Systems with Leslie Lamport | What's that Paxos thing all about...

Top 5 Tech Youtube Videos

I enjoy watching talks from various conferences and meet-ups. Here are some of my favourites from the past ~5 years:

  • James Powell: Does Code Quality Really Matter | Brilliantly funny talk. Other James Powell talks are worth watching too!
  • Distributed Systems in One Lesson by Tim Berglund | Insightful talk (frequently shared with Data Scientists upon being asked 'What is Hadoop'!)
  • Caching In: Understand, Measure, and Use Your CPU Cache More Effectively | Great talk for those interested in lower level performance
  • How to Speed up a Python Program 114,000 times | Slightly older talk but shows what is possible on a single machine, the presenter is also a funny guy
  • Java Futures, Devoxx 2018 Edition by Brian Goetz | What's on the roadmap for future Java releases and why
